Our Services

Introduction

We build high-end software for microcontrollers and
microprocessors using our extensive experience in embedded
hardware design and development, and our skills in RTOS
Communication protocols, networking, and security.

General

As an embedded software development company, we design and
code software for embedded systems from bootloader to application
or software for end users. We provide outsourcing embedded
software engineering services include firmware and device driver

Non-Automotive

We offer firmware development services for 8, 16, 32, and 64-bit
microcontrollers produced by the most popular semiconductor
vendors including Atmel, Microchip, Texas Instruments,
STMicroelectronics, Renesas, and others. We design both bare-
metal and RTOS-based firmware.

Automotive
